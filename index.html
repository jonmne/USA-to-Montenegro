About Jonathan

Not a blogger.
A qualified specialist.

I've been living and working in Montenegro for over six years. In that time, I've helped hundreds of individuals, couples, and families from across the world navigate the process of establishing themselves here — from their first question to their first residency permit and beyond.

"The difference between talking to me and reading about Montenegro online is this: I live here, work here, and deal with these processes every week."

My background is unusual — a degree in architecture, a foundation in business, and a career that moved through international travel, digital media, and eventually into financial planning and relocation advisory. The common thread is systems thinking: understanding how complex moving parts fit together, and how to structure decisions with long-term consequences.

I hold the Qualified Financial Advisor (QFA) designation from the Institute of Bankers in Ireland and the European Financial Advisor (EFA) qualification from EFPA. These are formal, recognised qualifications — not certificates. They matter because relocation decisions are financial decisions, and the two cannot be properly separated.

When you work with me, you're not engaging a blogger with an affiliate link. You're working with someone who is formally qualified, professionally accountable, and personally invested in your outcome.
